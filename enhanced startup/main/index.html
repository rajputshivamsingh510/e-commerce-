    Frequently Asked Questions

    How long does shipping take? +

    Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days within the US. Express shipping (1-2 business days) is available for an additional fee. International shipping typically takes 7-14 business days.

    What is your return policy? +

    We offer a 30-day return policy for unworn, unwashed items with original tags. Custom designed items cannot be returned unless there's a quality issue with the product itself.

    What materials are the t-shirts made from? +

    Our premium t-shirts are made from 100% ring-spun cotton, providing superior comfort and durability. We also offer tri-blend options (cotton, polyester, and rayon) for a softer, more vintage feel.

    Can I order in bulk for my team or event? +

    Absolutely! We offer bulk discounts starting at 10+ shirts. For larger orders, please contact our customer service team for custom quotes and additional options for team or event orders.

    How do I care for my custom t-shirt? +

    For best results, wash your custom t-shirt inside out in cold water and tumble dry on low heat. Avoid using bleach or harsh detergents, as they can damage the print and fabric over time.
